General description

Did you ever think you could play Super Mario Odyssey in your browser? Now you can do it... and in a retro version! To make it look easier, imagine it as a SNES game with the new mechanics: throw Cappy, jump on it, capture enemies and take control over them acquiring new skills and more.


Learn More

Controls

Move Mario with the arrow keys and jump with the spacebar
Press Z to throw cappy and capture enemies
Jump on cappy to get higher or press up arrow in midair to tackle
Crouch with the down arrow and turn into a ball by pressing shift while crouching

Features

Retro Plataformer
