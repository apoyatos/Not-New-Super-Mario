Description

Did you ever think you could play Super Mario Odyssey in your browser? Now you can do it... and in a retro version!
To make it look easier, imagine it as a SNES game with the new mechanics: throw Cappy, jump on it, capture enemies and take control over them acquiring new skills and more. You will be able to play the original game adapted to a 2D scroll aesthetic. Obviously, you need to collect all the power moons (not the 999 ones) and maybe you'll meet with unexpected enemys.
Stop reading this and go turn into a dinosaur with moustache!


Controls

Use the right and left arrow keys to move Mario.
Use the spacebar to jump. If you press the up arrow key while you are in the air, you can make one little jump.
Use the down arrow to crouch. You can move while crouching. Also, if you press shift while crouching and move, you will turn into a ball and go faster.
Press X to open the pause menu. Press Z to throw Cappy. You can jump on Cappy to go higher.
If you hit the enemies with Cappy you can capture them. Press Z to be Mario again.
